Toefl Writing Practice 3

Question: 　Do you agree or disagree the following statement: Talented people’s leadership is born naturally and can’t be learned by people.

Outline

  • agree
  • many characteristics, which leads to leadership, are innate. Such as extrem confidence in his idea, strong sense of control. Many people just cannot learn that.(Jobs and Wozniak, Larry Ellison)
  • Other characteristics, like intellectual quotient, the ability of fast learning, is hard for one to learn or immitate.

  • Common people can practice very hard to be a great leader, but not as prominent as those inborn leaders.

  • Hiring employees of high quality

  • concession: advertisement is important in branding a company name, making it well-known, but the core compatitiveness of a company is the product, businesses should make sure the product is good before put more money in advertising.
